PROJECT TITLE: BearCollectingFishes
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Lab 6
VERSION or DATE: Version 1
AUTHOR: Dino Angelovski

Description
In the BearCollectingFishes Game, you control a bear that collects fish while avoiding obstacles. The goal is to reach a score of 5 by collecting fish without running into Bombs. If you collide with a Bomb, the game ends.

How to play the game
Objective: Collect 5 fish to win the game.
Controls
Up Arrow: Move up.
Down Arrow: Move down.
Left Arrow: Move left.
Right Arrow: Move right.

Game rules
Each fish collected increases your score by 1.
Reach a score of 5 to win.
If you hit a Bomb you lose.

Bugs or limitations
Bombs may occasionally spawn close to each other.
